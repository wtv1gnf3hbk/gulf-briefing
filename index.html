Gulf Briefing - March 26, 2026

Ukraine President Volodymyr Zelenskiy has arrived in Saudi Arabia for what he called "important meetings" as he launches a surprise Gulf states tour to pitch Ukraine's drone expertise, according to Reuters reports.
