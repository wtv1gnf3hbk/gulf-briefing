**Country Watch**

**Saudi Arabia**
Saudi Defense Minister Prince Khalid bin Salman discussed Iran's "flagrant attacks on the Kingdom" in calls with Hungarian and French defense officials, according to his [Twitter posts](https://twitter.com/kbsalsaud). Foreign Minister Prince Faisal bin Farhan is participating in G7 ministerial meetings, co-chairing an international conference on Palestinian statehood with France, the [Saudi MFA](https://twitter.com/KSAMOFA) reported. **UAE** 
UAE air defenses [engaged 15](https://twitter.com/modgovae) ballistic missiles and 11 UAVs launched from Iran on March 26, the Defense Ministry reported, bringing the total to 372 ballistic missiles and 15 cruise missiles intercepted since Iranian attacks began. Senior advisor Anwar Gargash dismissed Turkish threats as representing a "new downfall" for Turkish diplomacy, [posting on](https://twitter.com/AnwarGargash) X that "there is no place for colonial illusions in this time."

**Qatar**
Prime Minister Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani condemned Iranian attacks on Qatar as "rejected and unjustified under any pretext," according to his [Twitter account](https://twitter.com/MBA_AlThani_). Foreign Ministry spokesman Majed Al Ansari met with EU Special Representative Luigi Di Maio, discussing de-escalation and freedom of navigation in the Strait of Hormuz, [per his](https://twitter.com/majedalansari) posts.
