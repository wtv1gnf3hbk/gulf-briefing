Top News

Reuters reports the visit comes as Ukraine offers expertise in countering Iranian drones, technology that has become increasingly relevant following Iran's sustained attacks on Gulf states. The timing is significant, with Saudi Arabia and other GCC members facing ongoing Iranian missile and drone strikes.

The regional security situation continues to deteriorate as Iranian attacks target multiple Gulf states. Saudi Arabia

Saudi Foreign Minister Prince Faisal bin Farhan participated in G7 ministerial meetings and co-chaired an international conference on Palestinian statehood with his French counterpart. Defense Minister Prince Khalid bin Salman discussed regional developments with Hungarian and French defense ministers, condemning repeated Iranian attacks. Ambassador Mohammed Al-Jabir posted that Al-Hudaydah is liberated, though this could not be independently verified and appears related to broader Yemen operations.

UAE

The UAE Defense Ministry confirmed intercepting 15 ballistic missiles and 11 drones from Iran on March 26, with air defense systems engaging targets across the country. Officials noted that since Iranian attacks began, UAE defenses have intercepted 372 ballistic missiles and over 150 drones. UAE Interior Ministry warned residents that sounds heard across the country resulted from both air defense intercepts and fighter aircraft operations.

Qatar

Qatar continues dealing with Iranian attack aftermath affecting its energy infrastructure. Prime Minister Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al Thani condemned Iranian attacks targeting Qatari capabilities as "rejected and unjustified under any pretext." Government spokesperson Majed Al Ansari met with the EU Special Representative for the Gulf Region to discuss de-escalation and freedom of navigation in the Strait of Hormuz.

Kuwait

Asharq Al-Awsat reports a fire broke out in a fuel tank at Kuwait Airport following a drone attack. Lebanon's Foreign Ministry condemned what it called a "terrorist plot" against Kuwait, according to Al-Ahram Gate.

Oman

Times of Oman reports two Omani women drowned in an Ibri water pool. Foreign Minister Badr Albusaidi met with the EU Special Representative, sharing "Oman's acute concern to bring peace to the region" and noting that "whatever your view of Iran, this war is not of their making."

Yemen

Saudi Ambassador Mohammed Al-Jabir claimed Al-Hudaydah is liberated and that "Marib War is in the hands of the Yemeni state."
